Laboration for Random processes
In the course Random processes there is one compulsory laboraration. This means the laboration has to be passed in order to pass the course. For making the laboration you need
    		• Laboration PM
    Text thoroughly describing the laboration tasks. The purpose of the laboration is to learn a little about simulation of a random process. This is carried out in two steps: first a few theoretical results are to be proved, then the actual simulation is to be performed by using a common statistics program package called R.
    and you may also be helped by
    		• Laboration hints
    Text indicating hints to solving the tasks, the theoretical as well as the practial.

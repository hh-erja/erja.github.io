Project paper recommendations

Some papers recommended for the projects are:
    		• Bayesian grid matching
    K. Hartelius and J.M. Carstensen, IEEE transactions on pattern analysis and machine intelligence, vol. 25, nr. 2, 2003.
    		• Robustness of the Markov-chain model for cyber-attack detection
    N. Ye et al, IEEE transactions on reliability, vol. 53, nr. 1, 2003.
    		• Discrete-index Markov-type random processes
    H. Derin and P.A. Kelly, Proceedings of the IEEE, vol. 77, nr. 10, 1989.
    		• A simulation-based estimator for hidden Markov random fields
    A. Verijanen, IEEE transactions on pattern analysis and machine intelligence, vol. 13, nr. 8, 1991.
    		• Hidden Markov random field model selection criteria based on mean field-like approximations
    F. Forbes and N. Peyrard, IEEE transactions on pattern analysis and machine intelligence, vol. 25, nr. 9, 2003.
    		• Hidden Markov model signal processing in presence of unknown deterministic interferences
    V. Krishnamurthy et al, IEEE transactions on automatic control, vol. 38, nr. 1, 1993.
    		• Hidden Markov models with states depending on observations
    Y. Li, Pattern recognition lettres, vol. 26, nr. 7, 2005.
    		• Survey of clustering algorithms
    R. Xu and D. Wunch II, IEEE transactions on neural networks, vol. 16, nr. 3, 2005.
    		• A study on IMM with NPHMM and an application to speech enhancement
    K.Y. Lee and J. Lee, Signal processing, vol. 89, nr. 9, 2004.
    		• Learning switching dynamic models for objects tracking
    G. Celeux et al, Pattern recognition, vol. 37, nr. 9, 2004.
    		• Simple models for relaxation in glass-forming systems
    M. Lopez de Haro et al, Journal of non-crustalline solids, vol. 329, nr. 1-3, 2003.
    		• Some recent developments in models with absorbing states
    M. Droz and A. Lipowski, Brazilian journal of physics, vol. 33, nr. 3, 2003.

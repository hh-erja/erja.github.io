as well as the text

Alternatively one may pay the union fee via the internet at http://www.karavgift.nu.
A few comments about the student union fee
In order to have results from examinations registered the union fee has to be paid. Observe that the union fee is to be paid semester-wise so the fee only has to be paid once each semester regardless of how many exams made.
Once having paid the union fee one may access a computer account at the university's computer network (with email, homepage and access to special services such as digital application for exams via the university's homepages and studentLADOK where one may check that passed courses have been reported) and a student ID card. Read more about how to get a computer identity at the university (in Swedish) and the student unions homepage about the union fee in English or in Swedish.

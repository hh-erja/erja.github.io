Signing up for exam in Random processes

Deadline
For passing the course the exam has to be written (with grade G [passed] or VG [passed with honours] or correspondingly with ECTS grade A, B, C, D or E). To write the exam one has to sign up before the deadline (look at the bottom of the page Tentamen for the link Tentor och omtentor. Coming to the exam without having signed up for writing it there may not be room for a place to write, so please do sign up before this deadline. For each course at Halmstad University there are three opportunities to write the exam (once as the course ends, and two more of which the third is usually in August the same year and the second sometime inbetween). The date, time and room is announced at the Tentamen page mentioned above a few weeks in advance.
Signing up
If one intends to write the exam at the university, one may
  • either sign up by sending an email to Eva Strid Andersson where one writes
    "I would like to sign up for writing exam for the master course Random processes" <course code> as well as declaring name and personal number.
  • or, having a computer account at the university (please contact Eva Strid Andersson about how to get an account), one may sign up via the internet

As examination part of the 7,5 ECTS credits Ph.D. student course Chosen topics in mathematics and statistics, each participant makes a project consisting of reading and summarizing a journal paper about some application of mathematics or statistic covered by the course and, optionally, explaining the relevance of that paper to the Ph.D. students own research. Each project amounts to a written report and and oral presentation. Here is the schedule of the presentations of these projects. Each presentation consists of a 10 minutes talk and a 3 minutes discussion.

Everybody who are interested are welcome to attend these presentations.

Presentations of the course projects of the
Non-linear optimization
January 29, 2013, Room E 202
Halmstad University
 
As examination part of the 7,5 ECTS credits Ph.D. student course Non-linear optimization, each participant makes a project consisting of reading and summarizing a journal paper about non-linear optimization and explaining the relevance of that paper to the Ph.D. students own research. Each project amounts to a written report and presentation. Here are the presentations of these projects. Each presentation consists of a 15 minutes talk and a 5 minutes discussion.

Everybody who are interested are welcome to attend these presentations.
